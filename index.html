About Ultimate Cars

Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts with over 50 years of combined industry experience, Ultimate Cars has revolutionized the pre-owned vehicle market. What began as a small family-owned dealership has grown into the region's premier destination for quality pre-owned vehicles, serving over 10,000 satisfied customers.

Our mission is to redefine the pre-owned car buying experience through transparency, quality, and unmatched customer service. We believe everyone deserves to drive a premium vehicle without compromise, which is why we maintain the highest standards in vehicle selection, preparation, and after-sales support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets your warranty apart from others?

Our comprehensive 5-year/60,000-mile warranty covers over 1,000 components with zero deductible and includes 24/7 roadside assistance, rental car coverage, and transferable benefits.

How does your financing process work?

We partner with multiple lenders to offer competitive rates and flexible terms. Our digital application takes just 5 minutes, with pre-approval decisions typically within an hour.

What's included in your vehicle inspection process?

Every vehicle undergoes a rigorous 150-point inspection by certified technicians, including mechanical, electrical, and safety systems. We provide detailed inspection reports and vehicle history documentation.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Yes! We offer competitive trade-in values and can apply the amount directly to your down payment. Our appraisal process is quick and transparent.

What after-sales support do you provide?

Our comprehensive support includes scheduled maintenance, priority service appointments, 24/7 roadside assistance, and a dedicated customer service team.
